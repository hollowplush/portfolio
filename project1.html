For my first project, I’d love to share this mage Kore piece. I like how it turned out and I loved working on it too! It was very fun :)

For those unaware, Kore is a character I created a few years ago based on the Greek goddess Persephone. It got wildly out of hand and I have multiple different universes for him, including a medieval DnD-esque one with lots of mages!

Here’s the sketch! Originally, it was meant to just stay as a sketch. However, I was bored one evening, so I ended up finishing it.

This is what the layers are of the final product. Part 1...

Part 2...

And part 3. I don’t name my layers, whoops… I grouped the ones that are about Kore. Anything below that group is background, anything above is foreground.

It was fun to draw this! Despite not having a full scenic art piece in mind when I created the sketch, I'm happy I was able to translate it into a full drawing anyway. Every scene I draw teaches me something new. In this one, I learned more about strong lighting.
